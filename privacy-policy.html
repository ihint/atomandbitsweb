Privacy

Privacy policy for Atom & Bits.

Atom & Bits, LLC keeps data collection limited to what is needed to operate the site, respond to inquiries, provide support, and improve products.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Information we collect

When you contact Atom & Bits, we may receive the information you choose to share, such as your name, email address, organization, role, phone number, project context, and message content.

For mobile apps published by Atom & Bits, app data may include local progress, settings, purchase entitlement status, crash diagnostics, and anonymous product analytics when those services are configured.

How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Provide consulting, advisory, or product-related services.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve websites, apps, and related systems.
  • Diagnose technical issues, crashes, and product reliability concerns.
  • Maintain business records and comply with legal obligations.

Information we do not sell

We do not sell personal information. We do not use mobile app data for third-party advertising, and we do not knowingly track users across other companies' apps or websites.

Mobile app purchases

If an Atom & Bits app offers an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. We do not collect or store payment card information.

Purchase status may be cached locally on your device so the app can restore or verify access to purchased features.

Third-party services

We may use service providers for app distribution, crash reporting, anonymous analytics, hosting, email, and business operations. These providers process information only as needed to provide their services.

  • Apple App Store and StoreKit for distribution and purchases.
  • Sentry for crash reporting, if configured.
  • PostHog for anonymous product analytics, if configured.

Data retention

Business inquiry and support records are retained as long as needed to manage the relationship, provide services, resolve issues, and meet legal or operational requirements. Local app data remains on your device until you delete the app or reset its data.

Children's privacy

Some Atom & Bits apps may be designed for broad audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it as appropriate.

Your choices

You can remove local app data by deleting the app from your device. You can restore eligible purchases using the app's purchase restoration flow. You can also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you previously shared with Atom & Bits.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Questions about this policy or an Atom & Bits product can be sent by email.
